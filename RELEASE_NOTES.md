# 🎉 What's New in WC2026 Predictor

## 🚀 New Features

**💰 Exact Score = 50 Points!**
Bumped up from 40. Get those scorelines right and crush the competition!

**🎯 Almost Correct = 30 Points (New tier!)**
Got the result right + one score exact? That's worth 30 pts now. Four scoring tiers: Exact 50 / Almost 30 / Result 20 / One Score 10.

**👀 Peek at Other Players**
Tap anyone on the leaderboard to see their locked-in predictions. Time to see who's actually good and who's just lucky 😏

**📱 Share Your Picks**
New share button with flag emojis (🇺🇸🇲🇽🇧🇷) — flex on the group chat when you nail it!

**📊 Result Accuracy Score**
New stat that tracks how often you call the match outcome correctly (separate from points). Pure football IQ rating.

**🔒 Locked Badge**
Matches show a 🔒 LOCKED badge after kickoff when ESPN hasn't gone live yet — no sneaking in late picks!
